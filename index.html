About the Network

The Emergency Care Network connects rural healthcare providers with urban hospital resources during critical situations. Our platform enables:

  • Faster emergency response coordination
  • Specialist consultations without patient transfer when possible
  • Streamlined patient transfers when needed
  • Secure sharing of medical records
